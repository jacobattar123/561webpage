Meet Our Dentist!

Dr. Stuart Price

Dr. Price is a responsible practicioner. He knows that dental proceedures can be intimidating for many people.

He always reassures his patients that the proceedures are perfectly safe and generally pain free.

In fact, he once even removed his own tooth, so patients can be sure that he knows what he is talking about.

You have nothing to fear in the capable hands of Dr. Price. Schedule a one on one consulation to discuss your individual dental needs today!
